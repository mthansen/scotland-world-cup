Kickoffs in Scotland time

Match control room

Kickoff times are shown in Scotland time. Live scores depend on public browser-accessible feeds. Odds are shown only when a source-backed public match-result price set was found before kickoff.

Odds provenance: Codex checked public web results on 26 June 2026 at about 22:00 BST. The displayed odds are limited to browser-accessible betting-preview prices found for Egypt v Iran and Uruguay v Spain: Egypt 7/5, Draw 9/5, Iran 13/5; Uruguay 6/1, Draw 3/1, Spain 4/9. Codex searched by fixture names plus odds, betting tips, predictions, and World Cup terms, inspected available result pages, and then entered only those exact source-backed 1X2 prices into app.js. No bookmaker login, live odds scrape, backend proxy, or betting API feed was added.

Senegal v Iraq odds were removed because that match was already ongoing during this update. Algeria v Austria, Colombia v Portugal, DR Congo v Uzbekistan, and Croatia v Ghana were left without odds because no reliable browser-accessible 1X2 source was found during this pass; earlier indicative estimates were deliberately deleted rather than presented as scraped or sourced prices. For displayed matches, app.js converts fractional odds into decimal odds, sums the inverse decimal probabilities to estimate the bookmaker overround, normalises each outcome back to a 100% book, and displays those implied probabilities in the match control room.
